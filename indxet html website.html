About Me

I’m an artist and photographer who loves capturing magic in everyday moments. Butterfly & Brush is where my creativity takes flight — blending art, crafts, and nature‑inspired pieces into work that feels alive and full of wonder.

I find inspiration in the small things — a flicker of light, a splash of color, a moment frozen in a photograph. My work is rooted in imagination, transformation, and the beauty hidden in ordinary moments.

Fun Facts About Me

✨ I have 22q, which means I see the world in a wonderfully different way. Colors feel brighter, shapes feel more alive, and tiny details often inspire my art.

✨ I notice things other people walk past — a shadow, a sparkle, a pattern — and those little moments often become my favorite creations.

✨ I love mixing photography with art, turning everyday scenes into something magical.

✨ Butterflies are my symbol of transformation — they remind me that growth can be beautiful and unexpected.

✨ I collect textures, colors, and natural shapes to use in my artwork. Even a leaf or a reflection in a puddle can spark an idea.

✨ My creativity is my superpower. It helps me express myself in ways words sometimes can’t.
